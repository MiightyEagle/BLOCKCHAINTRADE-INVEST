Your Questions, Answered

How does the semi-automated system work?

Our platform uses AI algorithms to constantly analyze market data for profitable trading opportunities. When a high-potential trade is identified, it's executed based on the parameters of your chosen plan. Our team of professional traders continuously monitors the system's performance and market conditions to make strategic adjustments, ensuring optimal risk management.

How do I earn referral commissions?

Our affiliate program is simple. Once you have an active account, you will find your unique referral link in your user dashboard. Share this link with friends, family, or your online audience. When a new user signs up using your link and makes their first investment, you will automatically receive a commission of 15% of their initial investment amount. There is no limit to how many people you can refer or how much you can earn.

What is the minimum lock-in period?

The minimum investment period is 7 days. After each 7-day cycle, you have the complete freedom to either withdraw your capital and accumulated profits or reinvest them into any of our available plans to continue growing your assets.

Are the daily returns guaranteed?

The percentages shown in our plans are 'Daily Return Targets'. While our AI is designed for high-precision trading and aims to consistently meet these targets, all trading involves risk. The market can be volatile, and past performance is not indicative of future results. Our system is built to maximize gains while minimizing losses, but profits are not guaranteed.
